SBQuest Community Guidelines

Rules for Safe and Fair Participation

SBQuest is designed as a safe and productive platform where users can participate in daily quizzes to improve their knowledge and learning habits. These guidelines ensure that everyone uses the platform properly and that all participation remains fair, transparent, and secure.

Participation Rules: Before attempting any quiz, please read the instructions carefully. This ensures that you complete the quiz correctly and follow the intended learning process. No external submissions, uploads, or personal inputs are required or accepted beyond the structured quizzes.

Usage and Safety: SBQuest requires login to track your quiz attempts and progress. Users should follow quiz instructions exactly as shown and avoid any third-party tools or manipulations. Misuse of the system may result in temporary or permanent loss of access to the platform.

Daily Engagement: Users are encouraged to participate in available quizzes regularly to maintain a consistent learning habit. All quizzes are displayed clearly, and users should attempt them according to the provided instructions.

Respect and Compliance: Users are expected to use the site solely for its intended purpose of completing quizzes. Any attempts to cheat, exploit, or bypass the system are not permitted. SBQuest reserves the right to monitor activities and enforce actions to preserve platform integrity.

Support and Contact: For any assistance, technical issues, or general questions, users should use the official Contact page. SBQuest does not allow user-to-user communication, ensuring that all inquiries are handled safely and directly by the support team.

By following these Community Guidelines, users help maintain a fair, transparent, and secure environment focused on learning and consistent participation.

Need help or have a question? Contact our support team.
