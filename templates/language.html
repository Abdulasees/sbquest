Introduction to English

Language is the foundation of communication. Learning English opens doors to global opportunities in education, business, and travel. It improves reading, writing, and speaking skills, enabling effective expression of ideas.

Tips to Improve English

  • Read books, articles, and newspapers regularly.
  • Listen to podcasts, movies, or songs in English.
  • Practice speaking with friends or online communities.
  • Write daily journals or short stories.

Interesting Facts

English is spoken by over 1.5 billion people worldwide. It borrows words from Latin, French, German, and other languages. Continuous practice helps learners achieve fluency and confidence.
