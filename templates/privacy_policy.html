Information We Collect

During registration, SBQuest collects only essential information required for proper platform functionality. This includes email addresses, chosen usernames, and optional profile details such as age range, learning preferences, educational history, subject interests, and skill levels. Gathering this information enables the platform to deliver customized quizzes, track progress, and provide appropriate content recommendations while avoiding excessive personal data collection.

Beyond registration, activity-related insights are collected. These include completed quizzes, scores, time spent on each module, timestamps for participation, and content preferences. This behavioral data allows the platform to refine recommendations, monitor engagement, identify knowledge gaps, and optimize educational pathways. Importantly, this data is exclusively used internally and is never shared with external advertising partners.

Technical and device-related information may also be recorded, such as browser type, device model, operating system, IP addresses, approximate geographic location, screen resolution, and session duration. This information enables cross-device compatibility, faster load times, and efficient troubleshooting. At no time is technical data linked to commercial profiles, sold, or used for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Functional Tracking

SBQuest utilizes cookies and similar technologies to improve usability, maintain session continuity, and safeguard quiz responses. Functional cookies enable accurate answer recording, prevent data loss during interruptions, and preserve consistent platform behavior across sessions. These cookies also store preferences to ensure seamless interaction during repeated visits.

Users may adjust cookie preferences via browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may disrupt critical functionality, including persistent login sessions, real-time progress tracking, and personalized content delivery. Retaining functional cookies is recommended for uninterrupted platform access. Occasionally, anonymized and aggregated statistics derived from cookies help the platform analyze trends without revealing individual identities.

Third-Party Services and Advertising

SBQuest may display curated advertisements from trusted third-party providers, including Google AdSense. These services might collect limited information via cookies to serve contextually relevant advertisements based on general user interactions. SBQuest has no control over third-party data collection; users should consult their privacy policies for details. All advertisements are educationally aligned, non-intrusive, and carefully selected to complement learning experiences.

Account Management and Deletion

Users retain complete control over account data. Account deletion requests can be submitted at any time through profile settings. These backups are purged according to privacy and security standards.

Users are encouraged to periodically update profile details and notification preferences to ensure stored information remains accurate and relevant.

SBQuest employs advanced multi-layered security protocols to safeguard user information. While no system guarantees absolute security, these practices significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access, breaches, or information loss. Users should maintain strong passwords, avoid sharing credentials, and log out after each session.

Security measures are continually updated to counter emerging threats. Vulnerabilities are promptly addressed, and users are informed about significant updates affecting their accounts. This proactive approach ensures safe interaction with quizzes, progress tracking, and platform features at all times.
