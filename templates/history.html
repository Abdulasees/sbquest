Introduction to History

History helps us understand how human societies developed over time through wars, trade, governance, exploration, and social organization. By studying historical events and movements, we gain insight into the causes of conflict, the rise and fall of civilizations, and the foundations of modern nations.

This section covers global conflicts, freedom struggles, early human societies, empires, and the exchange of ideas that shaped cultures and political systems across the world.
