Introduction to General Knowledge

General Knowledge (GK) is awareness of facts, events, and concepts across different subjects. It includes topics from history, science, sports, culture, and current affairs. GK helps improve intelligence, decision-making, and problem-solving skills.

Why GK is Important

  • Enhances awareness about the world.
  • Boosts confidence in discussions and exams.
  • Develops critical thinking and analytical skills.
  • Prepares for competitive tests and interviews.

Fun GK Facts

The Great Barrier Reef is the largest coral reef system on Earth. Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun. Knowledge is power, and staying curious opens doors to endless learning.
