Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is SBQuest? SBQuest is a platform where users complete tasks online and earn points that can be redeemed for rewards. It is free and accessible to everyone.

2. How do I complete a task? Browse the task list, click on a task, follow the instructions, and submit proof if required. Points are awarded automatically or after review.

3. How do I earn rewards? Each completed task earns points. Accumulate points in your account and redeem them for rewards available on the platform.

4. Is SBQuest free to use? Yes, signing up and using SBQuest is completely free. There are no hidden fees or subscription costs.

5. Can I use it on mobile? Absolutely. SBQuest is mobile-friendly and works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets.

6. How do I reset my password? On the login page, click “Forgot Password” and follow the instructions to securely reset your password.

7. How long does it take to receive points? Points are usually credited immediately or within 24 hours, depending on the task type and verification process.

8. Can I complete multiple tasks at once? Yes, you can work on multiple tasks simultaneously as long as you follow the instructions for each one correctly.

9. Are there any limits on earning points? Certain tasks may have daily or total limits. Always check task descriptions for details.

10. How do I redeem my points? Visit the rewards section in your account, choose a reward, and follow the redemption instructions provided.

11. Is my data safe on SBQuest? Yes, we take user privacy seriously and ensure that personal data is protected according to our privacy policy.

12. Can I invite friends? Some tasks or referral programs allow you to invite friends to earn bonus points. Check the referral section for details.

13. What if a task is not credited? Contact our support team with task details. We will review and resolve any discrepancies promptly.

14. How often are new tasks added? New tasks are added daily to ensure users always have opportunities to earn points.

15. Who can use SBQuest? Anyone over 13 years old can create an account and participate in tasks while following our terms and conditions.
