Top 5 Study Techniques to Improve Memory

  • Spaced Review: Review information at intervals for long-term retention.
  • Active Recall: Test yourself instead of re-reading.
  • Interleaving Topics: Mix subjects to improve understanding.
  • Visual Mapping: Use diagrams or flowcharts to connect ideas.
  • Teaching Back: Explain a concept to reinforce depth.
