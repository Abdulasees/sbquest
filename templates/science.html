Introduction to Science

Science is the systematic study of the natural world through observation and experimentation. It helps us understand how the universe works, from the tiniest atoms to the largest galaxies. Science is divided into major branches such as physics, chemistry, biology, and earth science, each exploring unique aspects of our world.

Why Science Matters

  • Advances technology and innovation.
  • Improves healthcare and medicine.
  • Helps solve environmental challenges.
  • Provides critical thinking skills.

Fun Facts

The human body contains approximately 37.2 trillion cells. The speed of light is 299,792 kilometers per second. Scientists continue to discover new species every year, highlighting the endless wonders of our planet.
