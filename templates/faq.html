❓FAQ

1. What is SBQuest? SBQuest is an Indian online platform where users can complete tasks and offers to earn points and rewards. It combines task management with a gamified rewards system to encourage engagement specifically for Indian users.

2. How do I complete a task or offer? Tasks and offers are listed on your dashboard. Click My Offer Logs to view available offers, or View All Tasks to see available tasks. For each task or offer, simply answer the required questions to complete it and earn points. No additional proof or submission is needed.

3. How do I earn rewards? Each completed task or offer grants points based on its difficulty or value. Accumulated points can be redeemed for rewards in the Wallet section.

4. Is SBQuest free to use? Yes, creating an account and completing tasks/offers is completely free. You only pay if a task, offer, or reward involves third-party services.

5. Can I use it on mobile? Absolutely. SBQuest is mobile-friendly and works on all modern browsers and devices.

6. How do I reset my password? On the login page, click “Forgot Password” and follow the steps. An email will be sent to reset your password securely.

7. Are there daily limits for tasks and offers? Some tasks or offers may have daily completion limits. The limit is displayed on each task or offer.

8. How do I redeem my rewards? Points are redeemed through the Wallet page. You can convert your points into cash and withdraw directly to your **UPI**.

9. Are the tasks and offers only for Indian users? Yes, SBQuest is designed especially for Indian users. All tasks, offers, and rewards are focused on India.

10. Is my data safe? Yes, all user data is encrypted and handled according to our Privacy Policy. Security and trust are our top priorities.
