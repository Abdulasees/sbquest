1. What is SBQuest? SBQuest is an Indian online platform where users can complete daily tasks and offers to earn points and redeem them for real rewards. It is designed to make learning and participation engaging and rewarding for Indian users.

2. How do I complete a task or offer? Tasks and offers are displayed directly on the website. Simply select a task or offer, read the given instructions carefully, and answer the required questions to complete it. Once finished, your points are automatically credited.

3. How do I earn rewards? Each completed task or offer provides points according to its value or difficulty. These points accumulate automatically and can be redeemed for cash rewards directly to your UPI.

4. Is SBQuest free to use? Yes, using SBQuest and participating in tasks or offers is completely free. There are no registration charges or hidden fees. Some external offers may involve third-party requirements, which will always be mentioned clearly.

5. Can I use it on mobile? Absolutely. SBQuest is fully mobile-friendly and optimized for all modern browsers and devices, so you can participate anytime, anywhere.

6. Do I need to log in or register? No. SBQuest does not require login, signup, or registration. All tasks, offers, and reward options are accessible directly without creating an account.

7. Are there daily limits for tasks and offers? Yes, some tasks or offers may have daily limits. These limits are not fixed and can change based on availability.

8. How do I redeem my rewards? After earning enough points, you can redeem them for real money, which will be transferred directly to your UPI. No personal details or account creation are required.

9. Are the tasks and offers only for Indian users? Yes. SBQuest is developed mainly for Indian users, and all tasks, offers, and UPI payments are available only within India.

10. Is my data safe? Yes. SBQuest collects only the minimal information needed to process rewards (your UPI). No personal data such as name or email is required. All information is handled securely in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
