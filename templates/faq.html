❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is SBQuest? SBQuest is an Indian learning platform where users take simple daily quizzes. It helps build consistent knowledge through short, focused activities on the SBQuest platform.

2. How do I participate in a quiz? Quizzes appear directly on the SBQuest platform website. Read the instructions shown on the page, select your answers, and submit the quiz once completed.

3. Is SBQuest free to use? Yes. All quizzes on the SBQuest platform are free to access. There are no subscription fees or registration charges.

4. Can I use SBQuest on mobile? Yes. The SBQuest platform works smoothly on phones, tablets, and computers without installing any app.

5. Do I need an account to access quizzes? Yes. Creating an account on SBQuest ensures safe access, organized participation, and proper functioning of the quiz system.

6. Are there limits on daily quizzes? Some quizzes may have daily limits to maintain smooth functioning and ensure equal participation for all users on the SBQuest platform.

7. Is SBQuest only for India? Yes. The platform is designed mainly for Indian learners, and quiz topics are based on Indian subjects and general learning needs.

8. How safe is my data? Only essential details are collected during account creation. All information is handled securely according to the SBQuest Privacy Policy.

9. Can I retake a quiz? No. Each quiz can be attempted only once to maintain fairness and encourage careful reading.

10. What topics do quizzes cover? Quizzes on the SBQuest platform include general knowledge, science, history, geography, and simple awareness-based topics suitable for daily learning.

11. Do quizzes have a fixed time? Some quizzes may include a time limit. If enabled, it appears clearly on the quiz page before you begin.

12. Do I need any books or materials? No. Everything you need appears on the SBQuest platform screen. No textbooks or external guides are required.

13. Can multiple people use the same account? No. Each person should have their own account to avoid technical issues and maintain fair participation.

14. Can I create more than one account? No. Multiple accounts for the same user are not allowed, as they may disrupt quiz flow or cause restrictions.

15. What happens if I face a technical issue? You can report the issue using the Contact page. Our support team will assist with login errors, quiz problems, or access issues.

16. Why can’t I see the quiz? This usually happens if you are logged out, the quiz window has closed, or your device is facing a loading issue. Refreshing the SBQuest platform page after login may help.

17. Why was my access restricted? Access may be limited if rules are violated, such as using tools, creating multiple accounts, or attempting to bypass quiz steps.

18. How often are quizzes updated? Quizzes are refreshed daily on the SBQuest platform. Each day brings new questions suitable for quick and meaningful practice.

19. Can I use SBQuest without internet? No. A stable internet connection is required for quizzes to load properly and submit answers.

20. Where can I get additional help? You can use the official Contact page to seek assistance. All support is handled directly by the SBQuest team for safety and accuracy.
