❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is SBQuest? SBQuest is an Indian online platform where users can participate in daily quizzes. It is designed to make learning engaging, consistent, and rewarding in terms of personal growth.

2. How do I participate in a quiz? Quizzes are displayed directly on the website. Before starting, please read the instructions carefully. Once ready, attend the quiz and submit your answers to complete it. Your progress is tracked within your account.

3. Is SBQuest free to use? Yes, using SBQuest and participating in quizzes is completely free. There are no registration charges or hidden fees.

4. Can I use it on mobile? Absolutely. SBQuest is fully mobile-friendly and optimized for all modern browsers and devices, so you can participate anytime, anywhere.

5. Do I need to log in or register? Yes, you need to log in to track your learning progress. Your quiz attendance and progress are visible only to your logged-in account.

6. Are there daily limits for quizzes? Some quizzes may have daily limits. These limits are in place to ensure a smooth experience for all users.

7. Is SBQuest available only in India? Yes. SBQuest is developed mainly for Indian users, and quizzes are designed for this audience.

8. Is my data safe? Yes. SBQuest collects only the minimal information needed for account creation and progress tracking. All information is handled securely in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Need more help? Contact our support team.
