⚙️ How SBQuest Works

SBQuest is designed to make daily learning and earning simple — no registration or login is required. Each visitor automatically receives a secure, anonymous Visitor ID stored in their browser session. This ID keeps track of all your completed tasks, daily offers, and wallet rewards without collecting any personal details.

  • 1. Visit SBQuest: Open the website to begin — no signup or email needed. A unique visitor ID is automatically created for your session.
  • 2. Complete Daily Tasks: Two sets of learning-based tasks are released each day in separate 12-hour slots. You can earn SB points by completing them correctly.
  • 3. Participate in Daily Offers: Learning-based offers are also provided each day, similar to daily tasks. Each offer includes educational or question-based activities that reward you with SB points upon successful completion.
  • 4. Earn and Track SB Points: All your rewards are securely recorded in the Wallet section, linked to your visitor ID — no account setup or personal data needed.
  • 5. Redeem Your Rewards: Once you collect enough SB points, you can redeem them directly through UPI. No email, registration, or password required.

Each visitor’s progress is stored locally using session data, ensuring full privacy and an easy, no-login experience. However, if you clear your browser data, cookies, or use a different device or browser, your Visitor ID will be reset, and your previous progress or wallet balance may no longer be available.

SBQuest automatically manages new tasks and offers every 12 hours, keeping the platform dynamic, fair, and engaging for all visitors.
